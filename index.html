Bio

Justin got his start in music at the age of 4 with a 1987 Yamaha PSR-2 which he still has and uses today. The keyboard quickly became his favorite toy and music never left him since. Starting his first band at age 12 Justin has been involved with countless music projects across many genres and at 16, he became obsessed with music composition. He was the first student at his high school to compose a full Big Band piece for the schools Jazz Band that was performed at a concert, as well as a 32-part wind ensemble piece. It was around this time Justin was gifted a demo CD for FL studio 4 and began experimenting with computer music. Virtually unaware of the world of EDM, a high school friend, after hearing some of his tracks, gave him the CD "Tiësto Elements of Life: Remixed". The record introduced young Mr.Waves to a whole new world music production and an interest in EDM. Justin learned to DJ in his hometown of San Jose California where he eventually threw his own party called San Jose Bass Club. In 2015 he moved to San Francisco and helped form "Subculture SF" a music collective that released music and threw parties in the city, booking notable acts such as Jai Wolf, Nadastrom, Kill Frenzy among others. After releasing a collaboration on Subculture called "On My Mind" which included his own vocals, his focus shifted to more vocal driven music. The goal of his new project was to completely go out into uncharted territory and tie his newly found voice with all of the production skills he has acquired along his journey. The result is a colorful tonal landscape that often follows a similar singer/songwriter format to artists like Rufus, and ZHU. Since then he has performed live sets opening for acts such as Telepopmusik and Kraak and Smaak as well as many pop-up DJ sets scattered around the city. Live sets employ a mixture of singing, live instruments and DJing, as well as live synth automation all made possible through Ableton wizardry.
