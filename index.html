Bio

Currently residing in Hollywood, CA, Justin Waves is an American singer/songwriter/ producer originating from the San Francisco Bay Area. Using a blend of analog synths, live guitar, keyboard, and his voice, Justin has created a unique strain of electronic music, flavored with pop sensibilities. With some songs akin to artists like Zhu, Bob Moses, and Rufus du Sol, Justin performs his sets live usually consisting of all original material including several covers like, Cher’s “believe”, and John Lennons “Imagine”. His live set debut took place in 2017 at the Great Northern in San Francisco opening for Telepopmusik, and has since shared stages with Shiny Objects, Kraak and Smaak, Pat Lok as well pop-up sets around San Francisco and several festivals. Currently, Justin remains independent, refining songs in the studio, and prepping for a proper label release.
