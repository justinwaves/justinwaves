Bio

Justin got his start in music at the age of 4 with a 1987 yamaha PSR-2 which he still has and uses today. The keyboard quickly became his favorite toy and music never left him since. Around age 16, he began his journey into computer music with the goal to make video-game music. Fast-forward 13 years later, the now 29 year old producer/composer has explored almost every genre of music before finding his current musical identity: Justin Waves. The goal of the project was to completely go out into uncharted territory and tie all of the production skills he has acquired along the way to his own Vocals. The result is a colorful tonal landscape that follows a similar singer/songwriter format to artists like Bob Moses, Rufus, and ZHU. Live sets employ a mixture of singing, live instruments and DJing, as well as live analog synth automation all made possible through Ableton wizardry.
